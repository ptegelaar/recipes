Ingredients:
 - 4 eggs
 - 4 slices of bacon
 - 1 tsp of grass-fed butter

Directions:
 1. Heat a frying pan, put in the bacon.
 2. When the bacon is crispy, put in the butter, which will melt within
    seconds.
 3. Put in the eggs, and turn down the heat. Scoop the bacon on top of the eggs
    to prevent from burning.
